About the Program

Bridging Strategy
and Execution

In a constantly evolving world, organizations need professionals capable of transforming strategy into execution. This Certificate was designed to strengthen that bridge — integrating project management excellence with strategic, financial, and human performance.

Delivered jointly in Brazil and Europe, participants develop a global perspective while mastering tools, methods, and leadership practices that drive organizational value. The program aligns with IPMA standards, preparing leaders to deliver measurable results through innovation, governance, and team excellence.

Program Structure

Four Integrated Modules

Each module focuses on a key area of competence, combining global content with practical application across 30 intensive learning hours.

01
Module 1 · Rio de Janeiro · Presential

Leadership & People Management

30 Hours · June 18–21, 2026
  • Stakeholder management and influence without authority
  • Behavioral profiles and emotional intelligence
  • Strategic communication, negotiation, and empathy
  • Situational leadership and constructive feedback
  • Conflict management and effective meetings
  • High-performance team development
02
Module 2 · Online Synchronous

Foundations & Strategy

30 Hours · Jul–Aug 2026 · Saturdays
  • Global trends and transformations in management models
  • The evolving role of the project manager
  • Strategic planning and performance monitoring
  • Portfolio and program management
  • Structuring PMOs and VMOs
  • Investment analysis and corporate finance
  • Governance, ethics, and sustainability
03
Module 3 · Online Synchronous

Methods, Tools & Operational Efficiency

30 Hours · Sep–Oct 2026 · Saturdays
  • Hybrid and agile project management approaches
  • Design Thinking and creative problem-solving
  • Decision-making under pressure: time, priorities, resources
  • Project planning: scope, schedule, budget, risk
  • Lean Six Sigma and continuous improvement
  • Integration of digital tools and platforms

Module 4 · Paris · International Immersion

Innovation & Practical Integration

30 Hours · November 2–6, 2026 · Sorbonne
  • Knowledge management in projects
  • Strategic positioning and institutional reputation
  • Customer journey and user experience

  • Project presentation and evaluation by expert panel
  • Personal and professional development planning
